ABOUT ME

NURUL SYAHIRA BINTI KAMARUZAMAN

'a girl full with challenge in life to faced'

Assalamualaikum and Hi everyone! My name is Nurul Syahira Binti Kamaruzaman, I am 21 years old this year and I'm truely Klantanese people. I am now doing my Diploma's in Information Management studies at Univercity Technology Mara (UiTm) Kelantan, Machang branch.

Describing Myself and i

Im the youngest sister from 4 siblings. Absolutely Im the crybaby one in the family, but i cant! Now Im 21 years old girl that can explore more in life. I love song but I can't sing anymore. I also love eating, but I can't eat a lot of food at the same time. Yeah! thats me.

Ambition and Dreams

I'm a young woman with big ambitions in the field of medical. These intentions, however, have been hampered because I moved my study focus to business and management up to this point. I have the constant desire to achieve my aspirations. I also want to study abroad in countries like Russia, the UK, and others. I have a strong curiosity about discovering and learning about the customs and cultures of all cultures.

Skills and activity

Information management is my major of study at the university. Through this process of learning, I was able to become expert in using a variety of programmes, including Microsoft, Adobe, Sublime, and others. Furthermore, I have the confidence to speak in front of groups of people. In addition, I frequently engage in voluntary activities like aiding flood victims, assisting numerous clinics in the department to conduct Covid-19 exams, etc. I also frequently work "part-time" in a watch shop.
